Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Offerz

Is Offerz free for customers?
Yes! Downloading and browsing offers is completely free. You only pay for the deals you purchase.
How do I become a vendor?
Download the Offerz vendor app, sign up with business details and documents. After admin approval (within 24h), you can start creating offers.
What if an offer expires before I redeem?
If you purchased an offer but it expires unused, we’ll automatically refund the amount to your original payment method.
Can I use Offerz without location permission?
Yes, you can manually select your city or area to see relevant offers nearby.
