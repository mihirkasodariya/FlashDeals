Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about your hyperlocal shopping companion

Is OfferZ free for customers?

Yes! Downloading and browsing offers is completely free. You only pay for the exclusive deals you decide to purchase through the app.

How do I become an OfferZ vendor?

Download the OfferZ Vendor app, sign up with your business details and GST documentation. Our team will verify your account within 24 hours.

What happens if an offer expires?

If you've purchased a deal but couldn't use it before it expired, OfferZ will automatically initiate a points-refund or original method refund based on store policy.

Can I use OfferZ without location?

Yes, while the best experience is with GPS, you can manually enter your area (like Vesara or Adajan) to see relevant deals in your neighborhood.
